Artist's of the month

Every month, our offcial website highlights the most listened to Irish tracks on Irish radio stations across the island of Ireland. Therefore, users of our website can eassily see the most popular songs of the Irish homegrown charts.

Top artists of the year

Every year around Christmas, we work with our colleagues at Iradio who help us achieve our artists dreams of getting their song ocross radio stations in ireland and even further abroad. Iradio play the top 20 most popular irish made pop songs of the year with cash prizes available to the artist with the top 3 most listened to songs across Ireland.For the artist who comes 3rd with the most listened to track of the year, they will recive a 5000 euro cash prize. For the artist who comes second, they recieve a 10,000 euro cash prize while the artist with the most popular song of the year recieves a 20,000 cash prize, and not only this but the opportuntity to perform it live on presenters Dace and Finnula's christmas special show.This prize was currently set up in 2018 and has been very succesful acorss Iradios daily audience with a daily average number of listeners of around 200,000 across Ireland
