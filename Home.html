Introduction

Welcome to the offical website for promoting new Irish pop music. if you are an artist that thinks you have what it takes to get your music into the top Irish charts, please contact us now. We are "promoting Irish music", a website set up in 2016 to help show Irish artists how far they can go. Artists who have worked with us

Some of the artists that we have guided along the way include the likes of Reuben Hester, Ruth Anne, Lydia Forde, Pastiche and Lea Heart.
